Lily Yassemi (B.S. Computer Science, Cal Poly Humboldt) founded the Society of Women in STEM to create an inclusive, student-led community supporting women and nonbinary students in STEM fields.

During her time at Cal Poly Humboldt, Lily championed equity in tech spaces through mentorship, peer collaboration, and leadership. Her work earned her the 2025 Outstanding Student Award, recognizing her efforts to build a more accessible and empowering STEM culture on campus.

Lily's vision continues to inspire the next generation of SWIS members to lead with confidence, curiosity, and community.
